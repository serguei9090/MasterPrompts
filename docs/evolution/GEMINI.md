# 📂 docs/evolution Context

## 🎯 Purpose
This folder serves as the **Strategic Growth & Self-Improvement Layer** of the Morphic framework. It documents the long-term vision for the framework's self-evolution, including strategies for contextual auditing, architectural refinement, and persona optimization. It is the roadmap for how the framework "learns" and adapts to new technologies and developer workflows.

## 🏗️ Architectural Role
**Strategy & Evolution Layer**. This directory houses the "Meta-Rules" for how the system's own rules, skills, and agents should change over time. It is the core of the framework's self-optimizing nature.

## 🔑 Key Files & Exports
- **`evolution-strategy.md`**: The master framework for the 4-phase self-evolution process (Critique, Bones, Mind, Skills).
- **`v2_backlog.md`**: A list of high-level architectural and behavioral improvements slated for future versions.
- **`triad-v1.md`**: Historical and foundational definitions of the Triad persona interaction model.

## 🛠️ Operational Logic
- **Reflection Loops**: The AI uses the logic in `evolution-strategy.md` to perform background audits and propose rule updates.
- **Context Decay**: This layer identifies and mitigates "Instruction Baggage" to keep the framework's context window lean and high-signal.
- **Persona Fine-Tuning**: Lessons learned from project execution are distilled here into improvements for the `.gemini/agents/` profiles.

## 📜 Local Rules & Standards
- **Proactive Growth**: The framework is expected to proactively identify areas for structural refinement using the `/self-evolve` or `/auto-improve` workflows defined here.
- **Minimalism**: Architectural changes must aim to minimize "Token Friction" and maximize search efficiency for the AI.

## 🔗 Dependencies
- **Internal**: Fed by `docs/track/LessonsLearned.md` and `docs/track/telemetry.csv`. Drives changes in `.agents/` and `.gemini/agents/`.
- **External**: Aligns with evolving best practices in the AI and software engineering ecosystems.
